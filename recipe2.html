Manti Recipe

1. Make the dough

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and salt.
  2. Slowly add warm water and knead until smooth.
  3. Cover and let rest for 20–30 minutes.

2. Prepare the filling

  1. Mix ground meat, onions, and potato.
  2. Add salt, pepper, cumin, and oil.
  3. Mix until juicy.

3. Roll and shape

  1. Roll dough thin.
  2. Cut into squares.
  3. Add filling and pinch edges.

4. Steam

  1. Grease steamer trays.
  2. Steam for 40–45 minutes.

5. Serve

  • Sour cream
  • Sautéed onions
  • Tomato-garlic sauce
