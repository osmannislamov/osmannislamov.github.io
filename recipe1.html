Preparation

1. Make the dough

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and salt.
  2. Slowly add warm water and knead until you get a firm, smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 20–30 minutes.

2. Prepare the filling

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground meat, finely chopped onions, and optional potato.
  2. Add salt, pepper, cumin, and oil/butter.
  3. Mix well until the filling becomes juicy.

3. Roll and shape the manti

  1. Divide the dough into several small balls.
  2. Roll each ball into a thin sheet (about 1–2 mm).
  3. Cut into squares about 4–5 inches (10–12 cm).
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each square.
  5. Fold the dough and pinch the edges together to form the traditional manti shape.

4. Steam the manti

  1. Grease the steamer trays so the manti don’t stick.
  2. Place manti with a little space between them.
  3. Steam for 40–45 minutes until fully cooked.

5. Serve

Serve hot with:

  • sour cream
  • onions sautéed in oil
  • or a tomato-garlic sauce
